Our story

Taverns-To-Go was founded in April 2020 as the COVID-19 pandemic unfolded. When all bars and restaurants closed, two Irish expats saw an opportunity to bring the bars to you. Over the past 4 years, Taverns-To-Go has built over 4,000 bars nationwide. We enjoy nothing more than helping our customers create the perfect entertainment space in their backyards.
